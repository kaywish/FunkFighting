Jason "Jamaica Funk" Downer

Owner / Head MMA Trainer

ABOUT ME

Born in Port Antonio, Portland, Jamaica, Jason specializes in teaching MMA at Funk Fighting Academy. From a young age, Jason was passionate about sports, excelling in track and field and football, where he earned the nickname “Jamaica Funk.” After stepping away from competitive sports in high school, he sought a new way to stay active — and that's when he discovered MMA. It quickly became a lifelong passion.

In 2016, Jason began training in Kickboxing and Jiu-Jitsu, eventually transitioning fully into MMA. His dedication was immediate and intense — training seven days a week with a relentless drive to succeed. Jason competed in both kickboxing and MMA events, but soon realized that his true calling was coaching.

In 2018, Jason began coaching students of all ages, teaching boxing, kickboxing, and MMA classes. He later expanded his offerings to include personalized 1-on-1 training. In 2020, he founded TrainedbyFunk Personal Training, helping numerous athletes win state championships, regional pro titles, and amateur championships.

In 2023, Jason fulfilled a lifelong dream by opening Funk Fighting Academy, a premier training facility offering a wide range of MMA classes and customized 1-on-1 coaching. Today, Jason continues to inspire, train, and lead the next generation of fighters with the same passion and commitment that fueled his own journey.
