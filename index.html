MUAY THAI + KICKBOXING

Muay Thai, the "Art of Eight Limbs," is a martial art from Thailand that uses punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes. Known for its intense training, it builds strength, fitness, and mental discipline. It's popular for self-defense, fitness, and competition.

NOGI BRAZILIAN JIU JITSU

NoGi Jiu Jitsu is a form of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu practiced without the traditional gi. Wearing rash guards and shorts, practitioners focus on grappling, submissions, and control. It's fast-paced, technical, and great for self-defense, fitness, and competition.

MMA + WRESTLING

MMA grappling class focuses on the cage grappling aspect of mixed martial arts; this class includes techniques from muay thai, wrestling, Judo, Jiu Jitsu and other forms of mixed martial arts. very beginners friendly and ideal MMA competitors!

KIDS-SELF DEFENSE KICKBOXING

Kids should practice self-defense martial arts for several reasons

  • Confidence: Learning self-defense helps children build self-esteem and confidence in their abilities.
  • Discipline: Martial arts teach respect, focus, and self-discipline, which are valuable life skills.
  • Physical Fitness: Training improves strength, coordination, and overall health.
  • Safety: Kids learn how to protect themselves and handle difficult situations, promoting personal safety.
  • Social Skills: Martial arts often involve teamwork and camaraderie, helping children develop friendships and social skills.
